Vikendi (Snow Map)

Vikendi is a snow-covered map that features a mix of open fields and tight urban areas. Ideal for mid-range combat and stealth play.

Sanhok (Jungle Map)

Sanhok is a dense, tropical island map known for fast-paced action, close-quarters combat, and vibrant visuals.

Erangel (Original Map)

Erangel is the first map of PUBG and features a balanced mix of rural towns, military bases, and open landscapes. Perfect for classic battle royale strategies.
